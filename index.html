About me

Hi, my name is Dr. Aditya Mishra. I am founding director of Wisdom Academy. I hold a Masters degree from Indian Institute of Technology Bombay and a PhD degree from University of Massachusetts, Amherst. I have a unique blend of experience of working in both industry and academia.

As a scientist, I am fascinated by the ability of computer systems to make this world a better place. I have done intensive research in the domain of smart grids and cyber physical systems to achieve sustainability. Over the last decade I have published dozens of research papers and have been cited by thousands of peer reviewed research articles.

At the core I am a teacher, who wants to simplify Computer Science and Professional formation for aspirants like you. I have 7+ years of extensive experience teaching technical courses to undergraduate and graduate students in science and engineering disciplines. During this time, I have advised several hundred students in their curriculum planning. I have led many research and engineering projects focussing on students’ technical and professional development.

And here I am! Wisdom Academy is here to help professionals, academics, and executives like you who believe it’s the right time to turn their dreams into real life. This is no coincidence. I have been thoroughly researching computer science, professional formation and eastern philosophy for years before putting it all together into these courses. I invite you to join me on this journey!

May the force be with you!
