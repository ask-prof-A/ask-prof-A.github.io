About Me

I am a Lead Research Scientist at AI2. My research interests are in the area of Generative AI, Interactive reasoning and Scientific Discovery. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Carnegie Mellon University in 2015, advised by Prof. William Cohen and Prof. Jamie Callan. I completed Masters in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay in 2007. Prior to PhD, I worked at Google R&D Bangalore for two years. Besides research, I love hiking, meditation, and music.
